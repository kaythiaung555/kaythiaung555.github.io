An essay is a focused piece of writing where an author presents and supports an argument or explores a topic, typically with an introduction, body paragraphs (using evidence/analysis), and a conclusion, serving to inform, persuade, or analyze, and requiring preparation (research/outline) and revision (grammar/structure) for clarity and impact. The word "essay" itself comes from a French term meaning "to try," reflecting its historical use as a trial or attempt to explore an ideaan idea.
